❓ Frequently Asked Questions
Is IDPDR a substitute for a doctor?

No. Every prediction, drug report, and recommendation is for educational purposes only. Always consult a licensed physician or pharmacist before making medical decisions.

How do I generate a Disease Diagnosis Report?

Run a diagnosis from the "Disease Diagnosis" page, then click "Generate Diagnosis Report" to fill in patient details and produce a printable/downloadable PDF.

How do I change my password?

Open the profile menu (bottom-left) → My Profile → Account Settings tab.

I forgot my password — what do I do?

From the sign-in page, click "Forgot password?" and follow the reset link.
